Crème Brûlée

Description

Crème brûlée is a decadent French dessert consisting of a custard base and a caramelized sugar crust.

The treat is traditionally served in individual ramekins.

Ingredients

  • Egg yolks
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Cream
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Make the custard.
  2. Bake and chill the custard.
  3. Caramelize the sugar.
